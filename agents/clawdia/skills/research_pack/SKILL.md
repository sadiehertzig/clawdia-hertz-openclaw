# Research Pack

## Procedure

1. Use `web_search` for 5–10 results.
2. Use `web_fetch` on top 2–3.
3. Return:
    - 5–8 key takeaways
    - Glossary of terms
    - Source list
    - 3 follow-up questions

## Rules

- Prefer high-quality sources.
- Keep summary skimmable.
- Narrow overly broad topics.
